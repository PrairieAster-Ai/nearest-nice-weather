No Competitive Moat

High Impact

Risk: No proprietary data, patents, or unique resources preventing competitors from replicating core functionality

Mitigation: Speed-to-market advantage, local community building, brand trust through consistent user experience

User Acquisition Cost Inflation

High Impact

Risk: Digital advertising CPMs rising faster than user engagement/retention rates

Mitigation: Focus on organic growth, community features, and content marketing vs. paid acquisition

Weather Data Accuracy

Medium Impact

Risk: Inaccurate forecasts damage user trust and platform credibility

Mitigation: Multi-source data aggregation, accuracy tracking, transparent confidence intervals

Big Tech Platform Changes

Medium Impact

Risk: Google/Apple PWA policy changes, iOS Safari limitations, search algorithm updates affecting discoverability

Mitigation: Multi-platform PWA strategy, direct traffic building, email/social user retention

Market Saturation Risk

Medium Impact

Risk: Casual users abandon platform once they've discovered their local area

Mitigation: Gamification, seasonal content updates, social sharing, geographic expansion

Ad Revenue Concentration

Low Impact

Risk: Over-dependence on single ad networks or seasonal advertiser pullbacks

Mitigation: Diversified ad partnerships, local business direct sales, premium feature upsells

Scalability Challenges

Low Impact

Risk: Platform performance degradation under high user load

Mitigation: Serverless architecture (Vercel), automated scaling, performance monitoring
